Features

Location Based

Uber links you up with rides closest to you, telling you how many minutes a ride is. There is also a live ride-tracking feature so you can see in real-time a ride getting to you

Safe Driving

Uber drivers have excellent driving records and they are well-trained to manage the ride professionally. The app shows you the make and plate of the car coming to pick you avoiding any confusion

Ratings

After each ride, you rate the driver and the driver rates you. This allows future drivers and riders to know where each person stands. The ratings are instrumental in improving the services on Uber

Music

Uber allows you to link up with Spotify and Pandora enabling you to listen to your favorite tunes while riding. The drivers are able to connect these apps to their phone giving you an enjoyable ride.
